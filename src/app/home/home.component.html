Monitor stress levels of the vegetation on your farm using Pluvial.ai Remotely monitor the crops and orchards on your property, spot check the areas of stress levels, real-time, and taking immediate necessary actions to eliminate the impact on the crop yield. Orchard stress during the crop year not only decrease the crop production but may impact the next year(s) crop numbers by quality and quantity.

Pluvial.ai does this monitoring with minimum or no devices on the field, hence no installation cost and no period maintenance.

Inconsistences

Pluvial.ai will monitor for inconsistences in the irrigation and alerts the growers and the subscribers, to avoid under or overwatering conditions. Consistent schedule and irrigation will improve the crop growth and quality, which will create more reliable food supplies.
